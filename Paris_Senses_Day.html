Senses Day in Paris

Morning Start — Coffee & Pastries ☕🥐

Café Saint-Régis

6 Rue Jean du Bellay, 75004 Paris

9:00 AM — 10:00 AM

Kick off your day with a classic Parisian café experience on Île Saint-Louis. This charming spot is famous for its croissants and excellent coffee — a perfect cozy start before your perfume workshop.

Morning Activity — Perfume Creation Workshop 🌸

Le Studio des Parfums

23 Rue du Bourg Tibourg, 75004 Paris

10:30 AM — 12:00 PM

Walk about 5 minutes from the café to this intimate studio where you’ll craft your own perfume with expert guidance.

Midday Stroll & Exploration 🚶‍♂️🚶‍♀️

After the workshop, enjoy a scenic 15-minute walk through the Marais district:

  • Wander Rue des Rosiers, known for its quaint shops and historic vibes
  • Stop by Place des Vosges, one of the oldest and most beautiful squares in Paris
  • Explore art galleries or small boutiques as you stroll

Lunch — Classic Paris Bistro 🥖🍷

Les Philosophes

28 Rue Vieille du Temple, 75004 Paris

1:00 PM — 2:15 PM

This bistro offers a classic French menu with a warm, local atmosphere. Try their onion soup or duck confit with a glass of wine. It’s nearby your afternoon walk and very well-loved by locals.

Afternoon Leisure & Scenic Walk 🌳

After lunch, wander toward the Place de la Bastille and then follow the Canal Saint-Martin for a relaxed riverside stroll. This area is lively yet local — perfect for soaking in everyday Parisian life. Maybe grab an ice cream or a coffee at a canal-side café.

Getting to Wine & Cheese Tasting 🍷🧀

Departure: Around 4:30 PM from the Canal Saint-Martin area

How: Take a taxi or a quick metro ride (Line 1 from Bastille to Palais Royal - Musée du Louvre) to the wine bar

Arrival: 5:00 PM at Ô Chateau Wine Bar

68 Rue Jean-Jacques Rousseau, 75001 Paris

Evening Activity — Wine & Cheese Tasting 🍇

Time: 5:00 PM — 7:00 PM

Enjoy a guided tasting of six French wines paired with artisanal cheeses and charcuterie at Ô Chateau. Learn from an expert sommelier about the regions and wine-making traditions.

Dinner — Cozy & Romantic 🍽️

Juveniles Wine Bar

47 Rue de Richelieu, 75001 Paris

7:30 PM onward

Walk a short 7-minute distance from Ô Chateau to this beloved wine bar with a warm, intimate vibe. Indulge in rich, comforting French dishes perfectly paired with their curated wine list.

Optional Late Night Spot — Parisian Nightcap 🍸

If you’re up for it, finish your evening with a stroll to the Pont Neuf bridge for a night view of the Seine, or grab a cocktail at the nearby Experimental Cocktail Club (37 Rue Saint-Sauveur) — a chic and cozy spot favored by locals.
